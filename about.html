Our Mission

Sports Made Easy is the multisport online concierge service that plays sports participation more accessible for everyone, anywhere. Sports Made Easy leads the industry with AI-enabled features which make the member's experience hassle free, while helping them to get the competitive edge in a most cost-effective way.

As a service provider such as coach, hitting partner, personal trainer, and racquet stringer; promote your services on our platform, you could reach new clientele. As a service provider, get real-time messages and bookings through our innovative app. For more information on how we can help you grow your business, please email our customer support.

We can offer your facility more exposure through a wide range of promotional benefits such as brand promotion, facility renting, and sponsorship opportunities to help drive business. If you're interested, please contact our customer support.

With our world-class customer service and a growing community of users, Sports Made Easy is the next up-and-coming mobile application for all your sporting needs!
